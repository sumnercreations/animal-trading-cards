Cheetah

The name cheetah comes from an Indian word meaning "spotted one."

Elegant and graceful best describes the cheetah. The cheetah is smaller than the lion and leopard, but by far the fastest at speeds of 70 miles per hour it can run faster than all other animals. Now restricted to sub-Saharan Africa, wild cheetahs once were found in most of Africa, the plains of southern Asia, the Middle East and India.
